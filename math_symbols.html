MATH SYMBOLS CLASSIFICATION PROJECT


The dataset included over 9000 images of handwritten math charcaters ranging from + to %. In order to classify images based on the symbol they contain we utilized YOLOv5 and Otsu's thresholding to find the bounding boxes of the images and label them. Our model achieved 96% accuracy.
